Welcome to **fronchfry**. This is the first post.

## What is this?

This blog is powered by plain Markdown files and PHP — no database, no framework, no build step. Drop a `.md` file in the `/posts` folder and it appears on the site.

## How posts work

Each post starts with frontmatter:

```
---
title: Your Post Title
date: 2026-04-18
description: A short summary shown on the index.
---
```

Everything after the second `---` is standard Markdown.

## Formatting examples

You can write *italic* and **bold** text, `inline code`, and [links](/).

> Blockquotes work too.

Lists are supported:

- Item one
- Item two
- Item three

That's it — simple by design.
